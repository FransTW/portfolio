About me

Profile

Hey! I'm Thijs, an eager to learn student with experience in team based or individual product design and development. As an Industrial Design Engineer, I seek to conceptualize and create sustainable, producable and viable products to improve the world. I have all the skills to work together with a team or individually and take an idea to a final product.

Personal

I was born on 1st of May 2002 in Assen. Of course, I really enjoy designing and creating. However, I have some other hobbies of my own. I play two instruments, bass guitar and piano. Furthermore, I enjoy cooking, making good coffee, analog photography and spending time with family and loved ones.

Bachelor Assignment

Graphic Language of Producs

MSC. Industrial Design Engineering year 2 Module 2 UTwente

Produc Life Cycle Management

MSC. Industrial Design Engineering year 2 Module 3 UTwente

This project focussed on learning and implementing PLM practices using the 3DEXPERIANCE platform. The project used a case study of an imaginary company needing to improve and optimize a wind blade and YAW assembly of a wind trubine. Trough this case study the PLM change process and different apps on the 3DEXPERIANCE platform were explored.
