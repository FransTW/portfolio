About me

Profile

An eager to learn student with experience in team based or individual product design and development. As an Industrial design engineer, I seek to conceptualize and create sustainable, producible and profitable products for an intended target (group). I have all the skills to work together with a team or individually and take an idea to a final product. During my BSc assignment I learned a lot about working for a company. However, I still believe I have a lot to learn about working within the industry.

Personal

Hey, I am Thijs, I was born on 1st of May 2002 in Assen. Of course, I really enjoy designing and creating. However, next to this I have some hobbies of my own. I play two instruments, bass guitar and piano. Furthermore, I enjoy cooking, making good coffee, Analog photography and spending time with family and loved ones.

Bachelor Assignment

Bsc. Industrial Design Engineering Module 12 UTwente

For my bachelor assignment I worked for and with Salland Storage to develop a box truck that would work with their StowBox. The StowBox is a movable storage solution that can be attached and detached from a trailer chassis. Here you can see some of the renders of the final design. On top you can see the StowBox on top of the subframe I designed to make the connection between the Chassis cabine and the StowBox. Underneath that you can see foldable stairs that can be used to get in the StowBox while it is mounted on the subframe. The public summary of this thesis can be found here.
